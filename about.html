Our Story

Redefining Luxury Events in Tirupattur

MinMni Decors was established with a mission: to redefine the landscape of luxury events in Tamil Nadu through bespoke styling and royal elegance.

From our humble beginnings as a specialized group of artisans, we have evolved into Tirupattur’s most sought-after luxury event management firm. Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every celebration is a unique masterpiece. By blending artisanal craftsmanship with state-of-the-art cinematic lighting and the rarest exotic florals, we transform grand visions into unforgettable legacies.
